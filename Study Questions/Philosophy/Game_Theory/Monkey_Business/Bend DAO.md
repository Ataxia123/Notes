# NFT Collateral

# [Bend Exchange](https://www.benddao.xyz/liquidity/buy-nfts) and Down Payment NFT Purchase

**Buy with Down Payment is an option on Bend, currently connecting 4 different marketplaces (OpenSea, LooksRare, Cryptopunks, X2Y2).**

Successful Down Payment Purchase is subjected to a _1% Down Payment Fee_ on top of the Ask Price payable to BendDAO. NFT listed on Bend Exchange will be charged _2% as Trading Fee_ to BendDAO upon successful trade.

[Down Payment Purchase on the 4 marketplaces](https://www.benddao.xyz/liquidity/buy/down-payment) will not be charged for the 2% Trading Fee. Full Payment Purchase on [Bend Exchange](https://www.benddao.xyz/liquidity/buy-nfts) will not be charged for the 1% Down Payment Fee.

Sellers on Bend Exchange ([Gitbook-Collateral Listing](https://github.com/BendDAO/bend-gitbook-portal/blob/main/nft-liquidity/collateral-listing-seller.md)): NFT holders/sellers can choose to instantly get up to 40% of the floor value of the listing before it even sells.

Buyers using Down Payment ([Gitbook-NFT Down Payment](https://github.com/BendDAO/bend-gitbook-portal/blob/main/marketplace/nft-down-payment-buyer.md)): The buyer may pay a minimum down payment of 60% to buy a bluechip NFT from major NFT marketplaces while initiating a flash loan from AAVE to cover the remainder.

An NFT auction will be [initiated](https://www.benddao.xyz/auctions/available-to-auction) **when the health factor is below 1**.

```
Health Factor = (Floor Price * Liquidation Threshold) / Debt with Interests.
```

More specifically, if the collateral has a liquidation threshold of 90%, the loan will be liquidated when the debt value is worth 90% of the collateral value.

_For example:_

_Assuming that the floor price of BAYC is 100 ETH when you borrow 40 ETH instantly in Bend DAO._

_If the floor price drops to 44 ETH, the 24h liquidation protection will be triggered since the health factor of your NFT-backed loan is below 1._

_Health Factor = (44 * 90%) / (40 + interests) < 1_

For more info, please read: [Gitbook-Auction](https://github.com/BendDAO/bend-gitbook-portal/blob/main/lending-protocol/auction.md) and [Gitbook-24h Liquidation Protection](https://github.com/BendDAO/bend-gitbook-portal/blob/main/highlights/48h-liquidation-protection.md).
