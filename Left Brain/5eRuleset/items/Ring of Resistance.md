# Ring of Resistance

*Ring, rare (requires attunement)*

You have resistance to one damage type while wearing this ring. The gem in the ring indicates the type, which the GM chooses or determines randomly.

| 8   | Psychic     | Jade       |
| 9   | Radiant     | Topaz      |
